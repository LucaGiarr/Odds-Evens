Odds & Evens

Instructions

  1. Set the WINNING SCORE which is the score at which one of the players wins, then click the START button.
  2. Choose ODDS or EVENS and click on the respective button.
  3. Choose a number from 0 to 5 by clicking onto it (hands).
  4. Click the PLAY button.
  5. Count the total number of fingers between you and your opponent.
  6. If the total number is odd, then the player representing ODDS wins, vice versa, if the total number is even, then the player representing EVENS wins.
  7. Wins the game whoever reaches the winning score (set before starting the game) first.
